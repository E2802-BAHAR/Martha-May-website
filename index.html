About the photographers

We are wedding photographers based in Hereford, UK and we have photographed over 300 weddings now. If you think about it, roughly there is about 10 (8-12) group images per wedding. That’s why we can say we have taken over 3000 wedding group photos so far.If you think about the number of wedding photos delivered in total, roughly 800 per wedding, that will be well over 240 000 photos.And we usually take about 5000 photos at a wedding – so that’s over one and a half million photos taken. That’s quite a good experience.
